StudyMatch

Topic

StudyMatch is a website where college students can find study partners or groups that are taking the same classes as them.

Audience

My audience is current college students.

Database

The database will store user profiles (name, year, university), courses, study groups, and scheduled study sessions. It will also store messages exchanged within groups and session details like date, time, and location (virtual or in-person). The data will be dynamically added to the database as users interact with the platform (profiles, group creation, session scheduling).

Design & Style

The design of StudyMatch will have similarites to other popular networking websites such as LinkedIn (navbar) or Discord (chat feature). StudyMatch will follow a modern aesthetic, using a cyan blue (StudyMatch title color) as the primary color. It is complemented by a warm coral (subheading color) for accents, a dark pink for buttons (button above) a soft gray (paragraph color) for text, and a light beige (background color) background to create a clean interface.

Scope

StudyMatch will have 5 pages.
  1. Home Page – Overview of the website, introduction, and button to sign up/login.
  2. MyStudyGroups – Allows users to see their existing study groups. Also has a chat with study group feature.
  3. Profile Page – Users can manage their courses, study preferences, and personal info.
  4. Search Page – Each course displays a list of available study groups with filtering/search options.
  5. Login/Signup Page – Allows users to make an account or sign in to save their profile, chat history, and study groups efficiently.
